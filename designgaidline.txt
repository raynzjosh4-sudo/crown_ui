📱 iOS-Inspired Design Principles
Before we create the components, here are the design guidelines we'll follow:

iOS Design Characteristics:
Soft, rounded corners (8-16px)
Smooth shadows (subtle depth)

Clean typography with clear hierarchy
Smooth animations (200-300ms transitions)
Haptic feedback (visual feedback on tap)
Glassmorphism effects (semi-transparent overlays)
Safe area handling (respect notches/safe areas)
Accessibility-first approach
